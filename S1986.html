Our Objective

The government is collecting feedback to understand needs across Health, Education, Religion, World Life, Games & Sports, Business, Transport, and Government services. Your opinions help identify infrastructure deficits and guide service improvements. By sharing your thoughts, you directly influence decisions that make life better for your community.

Sudden Death Rule

Any incorrect answer results in an immediate session termination and loss of current progress.

Anti-Exit Security

Switching tabs, refreshing the page, or clicking 'Back' will automatically end your session.

Earning Potential

Earn 10 KSh per correct answer. A penalty of -7 KSh is applied for every wrong answer.

Daily Restrictions

You are limited to a maximum of 3 questions per 24-hour period.
